Gaia

AI-Driven Subsurface Intelligence

Gaia is a space-enabled subsurface intelligence system developed by AX GAIA Intelligence. It fuses Sentinel-1 DInSAR satellite deformation data with on-site GPR scanning through artificial intelligence.

The system provides early-stage indicators that support engineers in planning geotechnical investigations more efficiently — before committing to drilling.

Contact

The Problem

Geotechnical investigations are accurate but costly and time-consuming. Engineers often need to decide drilling scope without early, site-wide insight, increasing uncertainty and risk.

What Gaia Provides

  • Early site-wide subsurface intelligence
  • Fusion of satellite deformation and GPR subsurface data
  • AI-based anomaly and settlement indicators
  • Cloud-based geotechnical report generation
  • Decision-support — not replacement of regulated drilling

Target Users

  • Geotechnical engineers
  • Construction companies
  • Developers
  • Infrastructure owners
  • Municipalities
