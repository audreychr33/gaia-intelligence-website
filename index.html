Gaia gives engineers early insight into subsurface behavior.

Gaia is a non-invasive, AI-driven decision-support system for construction and infrastructure. It brings site-wide insight by combining satellite-derived ground behavior with on-site subsurface sensing, then translating it into practical early-stage indicators.

How Gaia works

From data inputs to AI fusion to usable outputs — built for real engineering workflows.

Inputs Integrate satellite-derived ground behavior with ground-based radar data to build a comprehensive, non-invasive understanding of subsurface conditions.
AI fusion Apply AI-based modeling to identify and quantify relationships between surface deformation and subsurface structure across space and time.
Outputs Generate structured engineering indicators and site-level summaries to support early-stage planning and investigation scoping.

The goal: give engineers a fast, data-driven “first picture” of the site, so decisions are better informed from day one.
